About this Blog

I started this blog page about motorcyles to inform new riders about the potential dangers one can face on the road. Apart from the obvious dangers such as corners, pebbles, tar snakes, man hole covers, and other motorist, there is a danger that most motorcyclist don't think about or prepare for. Animals! One month into learning how to ride I crashed my brand new motorcycle when a deer jumped out in front of me. You can read more about my story here. This was a situation I never thought about but fortunately I went through the proper training beforehand and knew what to do even in unexpected events. My mission is to help prepare others in the same way I was prepared to help save lives.
